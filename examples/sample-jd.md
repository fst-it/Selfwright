# Staff Software Engineer — Platform Engineering
## Meridian Systems (fictional)

**Location:** Amsterdam, Netherlands (hybrid — 3 days on-site)
**Type:** Permanent

---

Meridian Systems builds financial data infrastructure for institutional asset managers. Our platform
processes 50 TB/day of market data across global venues and feeds proprietary analytics used by
portfolio managers and quantitative researchers.

### The role

We are looking for a Staff Engineer to join our Platform Engineering group, which owns the data
ingestion backbone, the internal data-as-a-product layer, and the compute infrastructure our quant
teams depend on. You will split your time between hands-on technical work and cross-team technical
leadership.

### What you will do

- Design and evolve the real-time and batch data pipelines that ingest, normalise, and distribute
  market data at scale.
- Lead technical direction for a team of 6 engineers: set standards, run design reviews, and unblock
  the team on hard architectural choices.
- Partner with quantitative researchers to translate analysis requirements into reliable, low-latency
  data services.
- Drive reliability and observability improvements — SLO definition, alerting, incident response, and
  post-mortem culture.
- Identify and reduce systemic technical debt; champion platform initiatives with a business case.

### What we are looking for

- 8+ years of software engineering experience, with at least 3 years in a senior or staff role.
- Deep expertise in distributed data systems: streaming (Kafka, Flink, or equivalent) and batch
  (Spark, dbt, or equivalent).
- Strong Python and/or JVM (Java/Scala) skills; familiarity with C++ for latency-sensitive paths is
  a plus.
- Experience with cloud-native infrastructure (AWS or GCP preferred) and infrastructure-as-code
  (Terraform or Pulumi).
- Track record of technical leadership: setting engineering standards, mentoring senior engineers, and
  influencing direction across teams — not just within your own.
- Domain knowledge in financial markets (market data, order book, trade lifecycle) is highly valued.

### What we offer

- Base salary EUR 120,000–150,000 depending on experience.
- Annual performance bonus (target 15%).
- 28 days PTO, plus Dutch public holidays.
- Hybrid working: 3 days on-site in our Amsterdam office.
- EUR 2,500 annual learning budget.
